Marketing teams build audience segments out of layered attributes, events, and consent rules. I turned that tangle of logic into a workspace they could read, configure, and trust — with an autocomplete search that showed what you were choosing before you chose it.

What I worked on

  • Segment condition builder with AND/OR/NOT grouping
  • Autocomplete search for attribute selection
  • Live preview of segment size before saving
  • Error and validation state design

The hard part

  • Boolean logic had to stay fully intact — no simplification
  • Non-technical users needed to own and audit their own segments
  • The UI had to communicate what a condition meant, not just represent it

3BB Member was already live for millions. My job was to work inside a large inherited file, find which screen was actually current, and ship only the flows where the migration changed the user's path.

What I worked on

The hard part

  • The app was live the entire time — no feature freeze
  • Working in an inherited file with multiple outdated versions
